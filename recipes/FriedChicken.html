Oven Fried Chicken!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, mince
  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 6 bone-in chicken breasts
  • 4 cups crushed cornflakes
  • 4 tsp dried rosemary
  • 3 tsp dried sage
  • 3 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degress Fahrenheit (175 Celsius)
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the sour cream and garlic. In another bowl, mix cornflakes, rosemary, sage, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Coat the chicken in the sour cream mixture, then roll in the cornflake mix. Arrange chicken on a baking tray.
  3. Bake for 1 hour and enjoy!.
